Find answers to common questions about using NZ Services Directory

Finding Services

Use the search bar on the homepage to enter what you're looking for (e.g. "plumber") and optionally select a region. You can also browse by category or region from the main directory page. Use the filter options to narrow results by provider type (Independent or Agency), category, region, and sort order.

Independent providers are sole traders or individual professionals who operate their own business. Agencies are companies with multiple staff members or teams. Both are verified and held to the same quality standards on our platform.

The Verified badge indicates that we've confirmed the provider's identity, contact details, and business registration. For trade services, we also check relevant licences and qualifications. This helps you trust that you're dealing with a legitimate business.

Ratings are on a 1–5 star scale based on genuine client reviews. Only users who have contacted a provider through our platform can leave a review. We moderate all reviews to ensure they are honest and follow our guidelines. Providers can respond to reviews publicly.

Yes! Searching for, browsing, and contacting service providers is completely free for consumers. There is no cost to find and connect with professionals on our platform.

For Service Providers

Click the "List Your Service" button on the homepage. Fill in your business name, type (Independent or Agency), category, region, contact details, and a description of your services. Basic listings are free. After submission, our team will review and publish your listing within 1–2 business days.

Yes. Once you have an account, sign in and navigate to your dashboard to edit your listing details, update services, change contact information, or upload new photos. Changes are reviewed before going live.

Premium Plans give your listing extra visibility. Benefits include a "Featured" badge, priority placement in search results, enhanced profile with photos and videos, and analytics dashboard. Plans start from $29/month. Visit the Premium Plans page for full details.

Sign in to your account and go to your listing settings, where you can deactivate or permanently delete your listing. Alternatively, email us at hello@nzservices.co.nz and we'll process the removal within 1 business day.

Account & Safety

Use the Report a Listing page to flag any listing that is inaccurate, fraudulent, or violates our terms. Our team investigates all reports within 24 hours. You can also click the report button on any service provider's detail page.

On the sign-in page, click "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. We'll send you a link to create a new password. If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact us.

We comply with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell personal information. Read our full Privacy Policy for detailed information on how we handle your data.
